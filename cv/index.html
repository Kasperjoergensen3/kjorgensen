Current position

PhD student, University of Copenhagen (Drug Design & Pharmacology) and DTU Compute

PhD on the FOREVER / FOREVER-OCT project (“Finding Ophthalmic Risk and Evaluating the Value of Eye exams and their predictive Reliability”). The project builds a large Danish multimodal eye-health cohort (fundus, OCT, visual fields, clinical measures) to identify risk factors for sight-threatening disease, with a specific focus on glaucoma and explainable, multimodal deep learning on population-level data.

My work targets multimodal models (fundus + OCT + clinical) and explainable AI for screening and risk stratification, co-supervised between UCPH ophthalmology and DTU image analysis.
